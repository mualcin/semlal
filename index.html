Celebrating
the amazing life of
Semlal Mualcin

December 5th 1964 - September 7th 2022

Tuanbia

Vantlang khuami a tleicia hna Pu Thawng Kam le Pi Bual Iang i an fapa pathumnak a si. Unau chuakkhat pakua a ngeih hna. Cu hna cu Pi Sung Ṭhai, Rev. Zammang Mualcin, Val Rual Hmung (tleicia), Pu Chan Lian Mualcin, Pi Tumhnem S. Mualcin, Pi Ṭha Cin, Pi Khin May Htwe, Pi Ngun Ṭha Len le Pu Mawng Ci an si.

Pi Tanki Mualcin he chungkhar an ser i fapa pathum le fanu pakhat Pathian nih thluachuah a pek hna. Cu hna cu Ṭialboi Thang Mualcin, Silvia SuiCinPar Mualcin, Phun Uk Lian Mualcin le John Rual Hmung Mualcin an si.

Kum 2000 ah Guam ah a rak lut i 2001 ah khan Dallas khua ah a rak phan.

A ngakchiat te in nu le pa nih innchungkhar ah rak bochan mi pa fel a rak si lengah khua le ram miphun a ṭanh taktak mi Lai pasal ṭha a si. A tuahsernak in u le nau, tu le fa chungkhat sahlawh rualchan hi a zohkhenh i a daw tuk zungzal mi a si.
